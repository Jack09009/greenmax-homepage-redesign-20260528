Welcome to
INTCO Recycling

INTCO Recycling is a foam recycling specialist, providing a total solution to EPS/EPE/EPP foam recycling. We manufacture and sell GREENMAX foam compactors/densifiers and foam recycling machine, buy compressed foam blocks, and turn them into frame products.

Every year, INTCO Recycling recycles 100,000 tons of waste EPS, which helps reduce 300,000 tons of carbon emissions. INTCO has become one of the biggest waste EPS end-user and recyclers in the world.
